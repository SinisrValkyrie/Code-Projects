Valkyrie's Famous I Don't Know Pasta!

There once was a chef named Valkyrie who, no matter how she tried, couldn't think of what she wanted to eat. So she took a chance and raided her pantry. She threw together a little bit of this and a little of that, and thus was born the I Don't Know Pasta! A delightful blend of pasta, veggies, and a light garlic cream sauce, this dish is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Served with a side of garlic bread. And if you're feeling adventurous, ask your server to add a protein of your choice! (We recommend the grilled chicken or shrimp!)
$18.99
